Axis Pro Controllers are a line of customizable controllers so that those with physical disabilities are able to get back in the game. The design process for these controllers can involve a lot of back and forth between client and engineer because each disability presents unique demands. This design app eliminates a few steps in process by allowing the client to send the engineer precisely what they want so that the engineer can focus on making their dream controller a reality.
