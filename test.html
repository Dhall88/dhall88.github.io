Hello! I'm David Wirhal and welcome to my ineractive portfolio! Scroll down through my timeline and lets build something!

Ah, such humble beginings. Fruit Fight is my first solo project as well as my first foray into manipulating a webpage using JQuery. Here I learned how to manipulate the DOM and really make a webpage come alive. Use funky fruit artillery to blow up your opponent before they get you!

Enter databases. Frontend and Backend didn't truly mean much to me until Expanded Pokedex. Built with MongoDB and Express, EP expanded what I was able to do exponentially and allowed my to save data from an API for later use.

Four heads are better than one and React is better than....not React. Plot Master is the product of a collabrative effort for three other wonderful developers and friends. Built with MongoDB and React, this garden plot tool allows you to plan our a vegetable garden and provides tons of useful information about each plant.

Now we are cooking with gas! Better Recipes is built with Rails and React and is the first App I've made that I use on a regular basis. A huge recipe database, no ads, costumizable timers and all recipe instructions, ingredients, and ammounts in one place.

Inspired by my family, for my family. Wirhal Family Utilties. At the moment, there are only a handfull of apps availible but one day this "Franken App" with be as wild and diverse as my crazy family. Each app presented its own challenge to overcome and really tested everything I've learned.
